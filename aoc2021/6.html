Part One AND Part Two

As always, read the problem text first. In summary, the objective is to model the growth of splitting fish. At 0 days away from splitting one fish becomes two. One of the new fish is 6 days away from splitting and another is 8 days away.

The output to part one is the total number of fish on day 80. Part two is the total number of fish on day 256.

Solving Part One and Part Two

SPLIT into COUNTIF

The only clever formula in this is actually from reading input and converting it into counts. COUNTIF works on a range. That range doesn’t have to be in the spreadsheet. It can be the output of another function like SPILL.
