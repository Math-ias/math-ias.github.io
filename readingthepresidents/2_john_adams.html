John Adams

On John Adams I read David McCullough’s tome “John Adams”.

This is a heavy book. Literally! McCullough’s book was so hefty that I couldn’t one-hand it on the subway. McCullough does NOT hold back sharing excerpts of Adams’s writing. The second president eventually dies July 4th, page 700-something. And I emerge victorious.
